**Solution:** Expo will ask if you want a different port. Say "yes" (type 'y' and press Enter).

### **Issue: Still see "Error 500" on phone**
**Solution:**

### **Issue: "Cannot connect to Metro"**
**Solution:**
1. Make sure your phone and computer are on the **same WiFi network**
2. Check that your firewall isn't blocking port 8081-8084
3. Try pressing `a` (Android) or `i` (iOS) to use simulator instead
